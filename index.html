About the game

"Sphere Strive" is a captivating game where players aim to push a ball strategically to earn scores. The challenge lies in navigating obstacles without pushing the ball off the edge. SphereStrive offers a thrilling experience that combines precision and strategy for an addictive gameplay. Can you achieve the highest score without risking the ball's fall? Play Sphere Strive and find out!

Hints

If you get too annoyed with sounds and music, just head over to menu and press red mute button to disable all sounds!

Once the ball falls into lava the game is over! To restart the game press "relaod" at the top right corner of the screen.

Points can be earned by pushing ball around the map!



About each element


Element 1 contains the follwing: basic shapes, lighting, textures, shadows and motion.

Element 2 contains the following: mesh manipulation, heigh map, enhancing enviroment textures, continued use of lighting and motion

Element 3 contains: moveable player mesh, player animation and player interaction/physics.

Element 4 contains: use of gui to create menu system, use of audio, transition from menu to scene, menu interaction and music/sound effect in response to scene events or actions.

Element 5 is a mini game!
